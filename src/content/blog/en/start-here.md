This is the first post on the new blog.

The old site stays online as an archive, but new writing starts here with a lighter stack, a more reliable deployment path, and a structure built for long-term notes.

## What I plan to publish here

- essays
- project logs
- reading notes
- experiments around products and AI

## Why rebuild

I no longer want to maintain an aging frontend stack for a writing-first site. Markdown, Obsidian, and GitHub Pages are much closer to the actual act of writing.
